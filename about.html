OUR STORY

Your Home in the Heart of Kathmandu

Hotel Milky was founded with a simple vision: to provide travelers with world-class comfort while preserving the warmth and authenticity of Nepali hospitality.

Located in the vibrant district of Thamel, our hotel has become a trusted destination for tourists, trekkers, business travelers, and families visiting Nepal.

From breathtaking Himalayan views to personalized service, every detail is designed to create a memorable experience.
